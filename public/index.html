David Guttman

Organizer, Maker of Things, CTO

Hi. I'm David Guttman, and I'm passionate about creating interesting things.

I am currently the CTO at Interlincx. I have worked and led teams at Disney Interactive Media Group, DigiSynd, and AT&T Interactive.

If you're interested in JavaScript and in LA you should come to JS.LA. If you're not in LA, you can see our previous talks on Vimeo.

Once a month, I host a gathering of hackers, makers, artists, and inventors to share their work and inspire each other: Spec.LA. If it's your first time, you have to demo.

A portfolio of my interactive artwork, and related tutorials, are at thhis.com and dry.ly.
